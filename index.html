How to Make Noodles

  1. Boil Water
    1. Fill a pot with fresh water.
    2. Heat the water until it reaches a rolling boil.
  2. Prepare the Noodles
    1. Place a packet of noodles into the boiling water.
    2. Mix in the provided seasoning or add your own spices.
    3. Let it cook for 3-5 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  3. Serve and Enjoy
    1. Drain excess water if necessary.
    2. Add vegetables, eggs, or cheese for extra flavor.
    3. Stir well and enjoy your hot bowl of noodles!
